A little about me, I've been a strategy and management with Booz Allen Hamilton for eight years providing laboratory, surveillance, technology, project and programmatic support to the CDC and public health communities. Currently, I serve as the program manager for Booz Allen’s Laboratory Quality and Biosafety work at the National Center for Immunization and Respiratory Diseases (NCIRD) at CDC. I also serve as the team lead for Booz Allen’s informatics projects at the Management Information Systems Office (MISO). I have professional and scientific laboratory experience encompassing the areas of neuroscience, molecular biology, genetics, and animal behavior. My expertise also include project management, process improvements, modeling and simulation, surveillance, analytics, and IT implementation.
